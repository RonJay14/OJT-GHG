ENVIRONMENTAL MANAGEMENT UNIT DASHBOARD

Consumption Trends

The Environmental Management Unit (EMU) dashboard provides a comprehensive view of yearly trends in resource usage and environmental impact. At the beginning of the year, there is a noticeable variation, which may be linked to seasonal activities or operational changes.

As the months progress, the data shows a steady pattern with minimal fluctuations, suggesting that resource management practices have become more consistent and predictable.

This stability could indicate the success of implemented environmental strategies or the reduction of external factors influencing resource consumption and environmental impact.
